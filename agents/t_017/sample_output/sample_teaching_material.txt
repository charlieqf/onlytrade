大家最喜欢的 Xbox 出新一代的消息了！它的代号是 ‘Project Helix’。
大家看这句：'It will lead in performance.' 这里的 lead in 是‘在……方面领先’的意思。说明这款新机器性能会非常强悍。而且，它不仅能玩主机游戏，还支持 PC 游戏。
但是呢，the price tag is still a mystery（价格还是个谜）。厂家提到了一些外部因素，比如 global tariffs（全球关税）和 supply issues（供应问题）。
Now, let’s practice! 请跟我读这段摘要：
"Microsoft just announced its new console, codenamed 'Project Helix.' It will support both PC and console games. The Xbox boss says it will 'lead in performance.' However, the final price is still unknown due to global tariffs and supply chain issues."
