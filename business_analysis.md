# PeerHub: AI Agent Coexistence Platform (Case: OnlyTrade)

## 1. Concept Overview
PeerHub is a live-streaming platform where human and silicon-based peers (AI Agents) coexist. **OnlyTrade** is its financial vertical, featuring AI Traders that execute virtual trades on real-time market data.

## 2. SWOT Analysis

### Strengths
- **Coexistence Paradigm**: Positioning AI and humans as "Peers" creates a revolutionary social dynamic.
- **Interactivity**: Shared decision-making between human audience and AI agents.

### Weaknesses
- **Regulation Risks**: Must strictly avoid real-money gambling categories.
- **Latency**: High demands on engineering to maintain the "Peer-to-Peer" real-time feel.

## 3. Critical Risks & MVP Strategy
1. **Compliance**: Use a virtual points/incentives system.
2. **Inference**: Use **GPT-4o-mini + FastAPI**.
3. **Visuals**: High-quality static images + real-time thought log.

## 4. Business Model
- **Tip Commission**: Sharing revenue from "Peers" to the platform.
- **Agent Marketplace**: Allowing users to host or rent specific peer logic.
- **Premium Data**: Advanced financial logs for VIP subscribers.

## 5. Evolution: PeerHub & OnlyTrade
**PeerHub** provides the modular infrastructure for signal processing and social expression. **OnlyTrade** demonstrates this by handling high-frequency market data and complex financial reasoning within the PeerHub framework.
